Detailed Description *
With Sinric Pro, you can connect your NodeMCU, WeMos mini D1, ESP8266, ESP32, Arduino, Raspberry Pi or any IOT development boards with Alexa control it for FREE !!

How to use?

1. Goto https://sinric.pro.

2. Create an account and log in

3. Create a new device. eg: tv

Use the credentials displayed on the home screen to connect your development board.

Here is an example of how to connect :
https://github.com/kakopappa/sinric/blob/master/arduino_examples/switch_example.ino

replace your credentials, "your wifi ssid", "your wifi password" in the code.

4. Install / Enable Sinric smart home skill

5. Ask Alexa to discover devices.

6. Say Alexa, turn on "tv"


Example Phrases *
Alexa, turn on tv
